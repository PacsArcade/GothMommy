## 🐾 Companions

Every outlaw deserves a loyal animal at their side.

**How to get a companion:**
Visit the companion shop. Press **E** near the shopkeeper to browse available animals. Each animal has its own skin options — pick your beast wisely.

**Calling your companion:**
Once purchased, press **U** to call your companion to your location. They'll trot right over. Press **U** again to dismiss them.  
You'll hear a notification when they arrive and when they're sent away.

**Tips:**
- Your companion won't follow you into interiors. Dismiss them before entering buildings or they'll wait outside like a good dog.
- If your companion seems to disappear, just press U twice — dismiss and re-call.
- Don't leave them in the middle of Blackwater. People will stare.

## 🪪 ID Cards

In civilized society, a person has papers. You are going to need papers.

**Getting your photo taken:**
Find the photographer NPC in town. They'll prompt you to pose for a photograph. A camera interface will appear — your character's face will be captured.

**Printing your ID card:**
After your photo is taken, you'll receive a `printphoto` item. Take it to the Identity Office to have your card printed. The finished card (`man_idcard` or `woman_idcard`) will go into your inventory.

**Why do you need one?**
Certain jobs, properties, and services on this server require identification. Carry your card. Don't lose your card.

**Locations:**
- Blackwater Identity Office — near the government building
- Additional towns coming as the server grows (Valentine, Rhodes)

## ⛏️ Grave Robbery

The dead don't need their things anymore. You do.

**What you need:**
A `shovel`. Find one at the general store or loot one from the world.

**Finding graves:**
Cemeteries are marked on the map. Look for the red circle indicators on the ground near grave markers.

**How it works:**
- Stand near a grave and the **Dig** / **Pay Respects** prompts will appear.
- **Dig (hold F):** Equips your shovel and starts a 20-second dig animation. Don't stop digging. What you find is the luck of the draw — a trinket, a coin, a ring... or just a rock.
- **Pay Respects (hold G):** Plays a respectful mourning animation. Nothing criminal about it.

**Rules:**
- Each grave can only be robbed once per server restart. You'll be told if someone already beat you to it.
- No shovel, no digging. The game knows.
- Graves are found in Rhodes, Blackwater, Armadillo, and Tumbleweed.

**What you might find:**
| Item | Notes |
|------|-------|
| Old Trinket | Sellable |
| Old Coin | Sellable |
| Tarnished Ring | Sellable |
| Gold Nugget | Worth a bit more |
| Just a Rock | You deserved this |

## 💋 The Parlour Ladies (& Gentlemen)

Some establishments in this world offer companionship of a different kind. This is a 21+ server. You know what this is.

**Locations:**
- Valentine Saloon
- Blackwater Parlour
- Rhodes Saloon

Each location has **two workers** — one for each preference. If one turns you away, take the hint and find the other.

**Requirements:**
- 🛁 **You must have bathed within the last 12 in-game hours.** Show up smelling like a mule and you will be told as much, loudly.
- 💵 **Cost: $15 per visit.** Paid automatically at the end.

**How it works:**
1. Approach the NPC. The **Interact** prompt appears when you're close enough.
2. Hold **G** to engage.
3. The screen will go dark. You'll see a message. Don't ask questions about the message.
4. When you come back, you'll be $15 lighter, fully healed, and **Well Rested** for 30 minutes.

**Well Rested buff:**
For 30 real-world minutes after a visit, your character carries a quiet glow of satisfaction. Check your status — it helps.

**Tips:**
- The bath requirement is real. Track it.
- If you're turned away due to gender: the other NPC is right there. This is an equal opportunity establishment.
- What happens in the parlour stays in the parlour. (The rats in the walls know better than to talk.)

## 🧛 Vampires

The night is long. Some people have learned to use it.

**Becoming a vampire:**
Find or obtain a `Strange Elixir` (`ricx_vampire_transform_drink`). Use it from your inventory. There is no going back.

**Sun damage:**
Vampires take damage during daylight hours (6am–10pm in-game time). Stay inside. Stay in the shadows. Plan your day accordingly — or don't have one.

**Mana system:**
You have a mana pool (base 100, regenerates slowly). Vampire abilities cost mana. Don't drain yourself dry.

**Bloodsucking:**
Approach a human NPC in the right conditions and you can take what you need. Costs 5 mana. Be warned: there's an 80% chance someone notices and the law gets involved. Pick your moments.

**Bat Transformation:**
Command: `/bat_t`  
Transforms you into a bat. For travel. For evasion. For the drama of it.

**HUD:**
Command: `/vampire`  
Toggles your vampire HUD — shows mana, status, and relevant information.

**Skill Progression:**
Vampires grow stronger with XP earned through actions. A rough ladder:

| XP | Skill Unlocked |
|----|----------------|
| 100 | Bullet Proof (partial) |
| 200 | Enhanced Speed |
| 300 | Night Vision |
| 500 | Faster Mana Regen |
| 750 | Stronger Bloodsuck |
| 1000 | Reduced Sun Damage |
| 1250 | Bat Transform Upgrade |
| 1500 | Double Sun Resistance |

**Tips:**
- Keep the `ricx_empty_jar` handy. Useful for collecting blood.
- `ricx_human_blood` can be used/traded — ask around.
- Law enforcement will respond to bloodsucking incidents. Don't do it in the middle of town at noon.
- Being a vampire is not an excuse to be annoying. Roleplay it properly.
